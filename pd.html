TL;DR Overview

Short version: this app only uses your Facebook info while the app is open. Nothing is stored, logged, sold, or shared.

Key Points

  • this app may read basic Facebook profile data when you grant permission.
  • All data is used only in real time to display features.

  • When the session ends, the data disappears from our side.
  • You can still email us to ask questions or request confirmation.

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how this app (“the app”, “we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use our Facebook-integrated application.

We do not store, sell, or share any Facebook user data.

2. Information We Access

Depending on the permissions you grant through Facebook Login, the app may temporarily access:

  • Basic public profile information (e.g., name, profile picture).
  • Email address (if requested and granted by you).

We do not intentionally request or use data that is unnecessary.

3. How We Use the Information

Any information retrieved from Facebook is used only to:

  • Display your data inside the app interface in real time.

We do not use this information for analytics, profiling, advertising, or any other secondary purpose.

4. Data Storage

We do not store user data. Specifically:

  • No databases, logs, or long-term storage are used.
  • No data is written to disk on our side.
  • No backups of Facebook user data are created.

All Facebook data accessed by the app exists only in memory during the active session and is discarded when the session ends.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your Facebook information with:

  • Third-party companies.
  • Advertisers or ad networks.
  • Analytics providers.

No data is transferred or sold to external services.

6. Security

Even though we do not store data, we take reasonable steps to protect the temporary information that passes through the app, including:

  • Using only permissions explicitly granted by you via Facebook Login.
  • Requiring secure connections (HTTPS) for communication where applicable.
  • Keeping the app scope intentionally limited to minimize risk.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not targeted at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store information from children under 13.

8. Your Consent

By using this app and connecting via Facebook, you consent to the temporary access and real-time use of your Facebook information as described in this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any updates will be reflected by adding a Last updated date above.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how this app handles data, you can contact:

  • Email: privacy@b4.fyi
This Privacy Policy is written to intentionally avoid persistent data storage. If the app’s scope is expanded in the future, this document should be reviewed and updated accordingly.

Data Deletion Policy

1. Overview

this app does not store Facebook user data. All information accessed through Facebook Login or Facebook APIs is used only temporarily during an active session . Once the session ends, the data is discarded.

Because no data is stored, users generally do not need to request deletion. However, this Data Deletion Policy explains how you can contact us and obtain confirmation if desired.

2. Data Access Behavior

When you log in with Facebook, the app may briefly access basic account information solely for demonstration purposes. All such data:

  • Is processed in memory only.
  • Is not written to any database, file system, or backup.
  • Is discarded automatically when the app session ends.

3. How to Request Data Deletion

Even though no persistent data is stored, you may still submit a data deletion request or inquiry. To do so, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@b4.fyi

Please include:

  • Your full name.
  • The email address associated with your Facebook account.
  • A statement that you are requesting confirmation of data deletion related to this app.

Since we do not store data, our response will confirm that no Facebook user data is held by the app and that no further technical deletion steps are required.

4. Automatic Deletion

Data deletion is effectively automatic by design:

  • All accessed data is temporary and exists only during the active session.
  • When the session ends, the data is no longer retained by the app.
  • No long-term logs, caches, or backups of Facebook user data are kept.
  • No third-party service is used to store or process your Facebook data on our behalf.

5. Confirmation and Response

When you contact us with a deletion-related inquiry, we will:

  • Confirm that this app does not maintain persistent user records.
  • Confirm that any data previously accessed via Facebook is no longer stored.
  • Answer any additional questions you may have about data handling in this app.

6. Contact

For all data deletion questions or concerns, contact:

  • Email: privacy@b4.fyi
This Data Deletion Policy is structured to satisfy Facebook’s requirements for data deletion endpoints for apps that do not persist user data. If this app is later modified to store data, this policy must be updated before deployment.
